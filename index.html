What data we use and why

Platform data

We access Instagram Business permissions and messages only in conversations you authorize. Used to qualify leads and send replies. We do not sell platform data.

Retention and deletion

Message logs stay up to 90 days for analytics and debugging, then are deleted or anonymized. You can request earlier deletion any time.

Data sharing

Only with subprocessors needed to run the service like cloud hosting. No ad resale.

User controls

End users can opt out by sending stop or any word you configure. You can hard block users.

Delete my data

Email salesfinderai@gmail.com with subject Data Deletion Request and your IG handle. We confirm and erase within 7 days.

Security and compliance
