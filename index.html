How it works

1) Connect Instagram or Email

Use Meta OAuth for IG or connect your email. We store a Page ID and token (or email OAuth) to reply where you approve.

2) Set rules

Choose who the AI can reply to, set guardrails, and enable human takeover. You can pause the AI anytime from your dashboard.

3) Qualify and book

We collect details, answer questions, and share a booking link or order confirmation. Opt in to notifications for qualified leads, booked calls/services, or disqualified leads.

What data we use and why

What we store (server-side)

We store only account information needed to operate the AI (e.g., name, email, username, and your connection identifiers/tokens for IG or Email). We don’t sell data. We don’t keep conversation content beyond what’s necessary to deliver your replies in real time.

Control & deletion

Pause the AI anytime from your dashboard. To remove your account information, email salesfinderai@gmail.com. We’ll delete it upon request.

Notifications

Opt in to get notified when a lead is qualified, books a call/service, or is marked disqualified.

Your channels only
