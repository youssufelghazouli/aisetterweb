How it works

1) Connect your account

Meta OAuth (Instagram) or Gmail for email.

Takes ~2 minutes.

2) Set simple rules

Choose who the AI replies to and who it doesn’t reply to, set an away message, and enable human takeover. Pause anytime from your dashboard.

3) Let the AI work

It talks just like you — or your perfect employee (no one can tell it’s an AI) — then qualifies leads, books calls or service appointments, answers FAQs, and can share quotes or order confirmations. You get notifications for qualified, booked, or disqualified leads.

Service booking front desk style
lead: hi. do you do catering for 25?
ai: we do. happy to help. is it pickup or delivery, and what date and time are you thinking?
lead: delivery, friday 6 pm
ai: nice. sounds like a big night. what are you looking to get? trays, mains, sides, or drinks
lead: two chicken trays, one veggie tray, rice and salad, and water
ai: noted. any dietary restrictions we should respect?
lead: two vegetarians and one gluten free
ai: thanks for sharing that. for that order the subtotal is about 660. delivery is 10. tax is 80.40. estimated total is 750.40. does that sound good to you?
lead: yes sounds good
ai: great. what is the name, phone, email, and drop off address?
Next the assistant sends a friendly confirmation and invoice, shares the delivery window, and records the order in your log. You can pause or make changes at any time.
Appointment Setter qualifies and books
lead: hey, I was wondering what your service looks like?
ai: for sure. before we get into plans, what is your story with fitness? maybe past sports, how you have felt in your body, or your relationship with food?
lead: I have been working out consistently but I see no results
ai: I hear you. that can feel draining when you are putting in the work and the scale does not move. how long has that felt stuck?
lead: idk maybe 2 years
ai: thanks for being honest. what do you think has been holding you back most?
lead: I think I have been bad at tracking my food
ai: that makes sense and you are not alone. tracking can help a lot. have you tried a food app like MyFitnessPal before?
lead: not really
ai: no problem. if I show you a five minute setup and a simple three question check in each day, would you try it for a week?
lead: yeah I can
ai: awesome. quick consent check. can I ask a couple rapid questions to see if we are a fit, then I will send times?
Next the assistant gathers goals and basics, offers two time options, books the call, and saves the transcript to your log for review.
