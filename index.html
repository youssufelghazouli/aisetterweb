How it works

1) Connect Instagram (Business)

You connect your IG Business account via Meta OAuth. We store your Page ID and a Page Access Token to enable messages you approve.

2) Set rules & exclusions

Choose who the bot can reply to, define opt-out keywords (e.g., “stop”), and enable human takeover at any time.

3) Qualify & book

AI Setter collects pain points, screens for fit, answers FAQs, and shares your booking link. All messages are logged for transparency.

  • • Consent-first: only responds where you allow.
  • • Opt-out respected instantly (“stop”, “unsubscribe”, “pause”).
  • • Human override: pause/resume anytime.
  • • Rate limits to avoid spammy behavior.
  • • Clear identity: messages indicate they’re from your coaching brand’s assistant.

What data we use & why

Platform Data (from Meta)

We access your Instagram Business messaging permissions (Page ID + Page Access Token) and messages in conversations you authorize. We use this solely to qualify leads and send replies on your behalf. We do not sell Platform Data.

Retention & deletion

Message logs are retained for up to 90 days for analytics, debugging, and compliance, then deleted or anonymized. You can request earlier deletion at any time.

Data sharing

We only share data with subprocessors required to provide the service (e.g., cloud hosting). No third-party advertising or resale.

User controls

End users can opt out by sending “stop”, “unsubscribe”, or any configured keyword. You can add users to a hard blocklist in your dashboard.

Data deletion instructions

To delete your data, email salesfinderai@gmail.com with subject “Data Deletion Request” and your IG handle. We will confirm and erase within 7 days.

Security & Compliance

  • • Tokens stored server-side; never exposed client-side.
  • • HTTPS enforced; secrets kept in environment variables.
  • • Principle of least privilege; scoped access only.
  • • Audit logs for message activity and admin actions.
  • • Immediate token revocation upon disconnect.

We use Meta’s Graph API within allowed use cases. Our responses make it clear the assistant represents your coaching brand. We comply with rate limits, accept blocks, and respect user privacy.
